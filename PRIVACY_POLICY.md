# Zikirmatik - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 13, 2026

## Overview

Zikirmatik ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. **Local Data (Device Storage)**
- **Counter values and history:** Saved locally on your device using SharedPreferences
- **User preferences:** Language, theme, vibration, sound settings
- **Custom zikr data:** Your personal zikir entries (stored locally only)
- **No cloud sync:** All data remains on your device; we do not transmit it to our servers

### 2. **AdMob (Google Mobile Ads)**
When you use Zikirmatik, Google may collect:
- **Non-personal identifiers:** Advertising ID, device information
- **Usage data:** App interactions for serving targeted ads
- **Location:** Approximate location (if enabled on your device)

See Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:
1. **Deliver the app:** Display counter, settings, and zikir features
2. **Improve experience:** Analyze app performance and crashes
3. **Serve ads:** Google AdMob displays relevant banner advertisements
4. **Compliance:** Meet legal and regulatory requirements

## Data Storage & Security

- **Local storage:** All your counter data and preferences are stored only on your device
- **No transmission:** We do not send your zikir data to external servers
- **No backup:** Uninstalling the app will remove all stored data
- **Security:** Device-level encryption (as provided by your OS)

## Third-Party Services

### Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)
- AdMob may track ad impressions and interactions
- Google's data practices: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Delete data:** Uninstall the app or clear app data in Settings
- **Control ads:** Adjust ad personalization in Google My Ad Center (https://myadcenter.google.com)
- **Data access:** Request a copy of your data (does not apply; no cloud storage)

## Children's Privacy

Zikirmatik is intended for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such information, we will delete it immediately.
